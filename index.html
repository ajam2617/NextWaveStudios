    Our Process

    We Make Our Customers Happy

    We’re here to serve you. It is our goal to make our customers happy and on their way to thrive out in the interwebs. Let us be transparent with you and describe our process when it comes to creating the product that the customer desires.

    Concept

    We’re in this together, therefore we need your input in conceptualizing what you’re trying to achieve and how Next Wave Studios can make it happen. Look at this as a collaboration.

    Research

    We at Next Wave Studios will do our homework to find the best approach to achieve our product that will be beyond satisfactory for our client.

    Develop

    This is when we get the cooks into the kitchen and work tirelessly to achieve the goal of the client. This will start with whiteboarding with the team and then diving head first into the code.

    Test

    Nothing great comes easy. In order to achieve greatness, we have to test our product with rigorous repetition, in order to work out the kinks.

    Finally, we end our process making sure the client is happy with the product we’re giving them. If that’s not the case, then our process is not done. If our client is happy, then we’re happy.
